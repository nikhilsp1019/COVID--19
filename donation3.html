About Sharan for Animals

Our activities are our frontline offensive against animal cruelty in Nashik and beyond. They are our gateway to a humane future for animals.

Animal Rescues
  • We take up animal rescue operations of all scales across the streets of Nashik.

    • Medical Treatment
  • Your donations help us to take up medical care and major surgeries of at-risk animals.

    • Animal Shelter
  • Our shelter is home to tens and hundreds of rescued animals waiting to receive your love
    • An Animal Lover’s Paradise

    Our animal shelter in Nashik is a pleasant and cheerful place to be. It’s a place full of hope and opportunities to be around loving animals and animal lovers. Dogs (a lot of them!), cats, cows, monkeys, owls (yes, for real!), horses, and more; our all-animal shelter is home to more than a 100 loving animals waiting to be found by their new, caring family.


    Whether you’re an animal lover from Nashik who wants to escape into an animal paradise or you’re visiting the wine capital and want to experience more than just the ordinary; Sharan For Animals is a life-changing experience for both the visitors, as well as its four-legged residents.


    Come be a part of SHARAN, our animal shelter is located just outside of Nashik City in the village of Vilholi which falls in the way if you’re driving down from Mumbai.
